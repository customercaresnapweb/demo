Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to see Dr. Carter?

No referral is strictly necessary for cosmetic consultations. However, for medical insurance claims regarding clinical dermatology, a referral might be required by your provider.

Are online consultations effective for skin conditions?

Yes, many conditions like acne, rosacea, and mild eczema can be diagnosed and managed effectively via our secure high-resolution tele-dermatology platform.

Does laser hair removal work on all skin types?

We use the latest dual-wavelength laser technology, allowing us to safely and effectively treat all Fitzpatrick skin types, from very fair to deeply pigmented skin.

Is there downtime after anti-aging injections?

Most neurotoxin injections have zero downtime. Dermal fillers may cause slight swelling or bruising for 24-48 hours, but you can generally resume normal activities immediately.

How often should I get a skin cancer screening?

We recommend an annual full-body mole check for most patients, and every 6 months for individuals with a family history of melanoma or extensive sun damage.
